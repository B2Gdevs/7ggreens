UNCLE PAUL’S AGRITOURISM & EDUCATIONAL CORP. WEB INFORMATION
WELCOME TO Uncle Paul’s Agritourism & Educational Corp (UPAEC)
Thank you for visiting UPAEC website. We believed that the universe entrusted us to be good stewards of the world including the land. The land is what provides food, shelter, and clothing. Through observation I learn that the land keeps giving according to how you communicate with it. 
**ABOUT**** US**
UPAEC was founded in 2019 with the goal of delivering high-quality, nutritious fruits and vegetables to communities in Dallas-Fort Worth and East Texas.  
Our farm is named in honor of my father Paul affectionately called Uncle Paul who operated fruit, vegetable chicken farm, and agri-logistics. In my later years, I developed a strong interest in delivering farm-fresh produce to the local community and ensuring access to high-quality food sources.  I went from gardening as therapeutic to farming as a career. 
I loved eating fresh fruits and vegetables, watch nature do its thing, and the plants grow using natural practices as nature intended without chemicals and synthetic pesticides. We have spent multiple years learning all we could about sustainable agriculture; through our years of study, we realized that we can do farming without chemicals by letting nature fertilize, protect, and grow with some assistance from us.
We recognize that the food we should contain is the nourishment that our bodies need. We 
At UPAEC, we want to make this nourishment readily available and provide it to our communities. In addition to providing nutritious sustenance to the community, we desire to regenerate the soil using nature to create growth in the soil and food we produce on our farm.
**WHO WE ARE**
UPAEC is a farm in the East Texas rural community. We grow and produce our fruits and vegetables without chemical or pesticides. Our fruits and vegetables are planted in the natural fertile ground, and fabric containers absorb and replenish the fields with sustainable natural minerals and nutrients. Our seeds are not GMOs. UPAEC exists to nourish our community with produce grown to the highest standards. Our promise is simple: if we wouldn't eat it, we won't sell it."
**WHAT WE DO**
We focus on providing food for the East Texas, Dallas-Fort Worth communities. We use sustainable holistic management practices, produce safety management and our mobile cold chain management practices to reduce waste while offering you high quality products.
Produce is picked after pre-orders to reduce waste and support sustainability.
- Harvested produce is moved to our temperature-controlled cooler within an hour. 
- We are certified PSA and follow the guidelines in our packaging process.
- We deliver our produce from UPAEC’s refrigerated truck to ensure temperature safe from the farm to the delivery point.
- We support local farmers.
We provide chemical free and non-GMO produce
**OUR PRACTICERS**
At UPAEC we are intentional about how we grow and produce food and stewardship to the land, so we do not use chemicals, synthetic fertilizers, or pesticides. We work with the land and provide natural nutrients

What is Your Choice Box?
Your Choice Box is different from Community Supported Agriculture (CSA). It means buying your groceries whenever it suits you and collecting them at a time that works best for your schedule.
