# Keeping It Fresh - Field to Family

***Keeping It Fresh, From Field to Family***
At Uncle Paul's Agritourism & Educational Corp, (UPAEC) we believe the journey from harvest to your hands matters just as much as the harvest itself.
That's why we invested in our mobile cold chain, a walk-in cooler and refrigerated delivery truck working together as one seamless system. From the moment our vegetables are picked, they move into a controlled, temperature-safe environment that locks in freshness, preserves nutrition, and ensures every product leaving our farm meets the highest safety standards.
No warm gaps. No guesswork. Just clean, cold, consistent care from our fields to your door.
Our cold chain isn't just equipment, it's our promise. A promise that the same dedication we put into growing wholesome produce is carried all the way through to delivery. Whether we're supplying fresh greens or frozen vegetables, UPAEC's mobile cold chain means our customers, partners, and community can always trust what's inside the box.
