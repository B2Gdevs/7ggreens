# 2S Ranch — Visual Design Reference

## Aesthetic
Rustic, heritage-focused ranching aesthetic with earthy authenticity. Photo-led storytelling emphasizing open pastures and ranch life, paired with clean typography and a warm, natural color palette. Evokes "the way ranching used to be" through authentic imagery and direct messaging.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Neutrals:** cream / off-white backgrounds, deep charcoal text
- **Accent:** sage green, warm tan / gold tones (inferred from imagery)
- **Photography Tones:** golden-hour lighting, natural greens, weathered wood textures

## Typography
- **Headings:** sans-serif, bold weight, generous letter-spacing — modern but approachable
- **Body:** clean sans-serif, moderate line-height
- **Tone:** direct, conversational, values-driven (e.g. "Healthy Land. Healthy Cows. Healthy People.")

## Header & Navigation
- Sticky horizontal nav, logo left-aligned
- Primary links: Our Story, Experiences, Wholesale, Contact
- Secondary CTA: external link to The Provision House
- Cart icon top-right (commerce integration)
- Mobile menu toggle

## Hero
Full-width hero image of grazing cattle with tagline overlay. Bold heading + dual CTAs ("Buy our beef").

## Page Sections (in order)
1. Navigation header
2. Hero image + tagline
3. "The 2S Difference" — 4-column feature grid with icons
4. Founder story with portrait
5. "Our Purpose" narrative section
6. The Provision House feature
7. Founder quote callout
8. Ranch-to-Table Dinners
9. The Ranch Experience
10. Thank-you / community message
11. Instagram feed gallery (6 images)
12. Footer with logo, links, contact info

## Buttons
- Primary CTA: solid fill, generous padding, likely sage/earth-tone background
- Secondary: text-based or outlined variants
- Consistent hover states implied

## Photography
Authentic documentary-style ranch imagery: cowboys on horseback, cattle herds, prepared meals, outdoor gatherings. Golden-hour preferred; focus on people-land-food connection.
